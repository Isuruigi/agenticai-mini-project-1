Foreign Currency 
The functional currency of our foreign subsidiaries is the local currency or U.S. dollar depending on the nature of the subsidiaries’ 
activities. Monetary assets and liabilities denominated in currencies other than the functional currency are remeasured to the functional 
currency at the exchange rate in effect at the end of the period. Gains and losses resulting from remeasurement are recorded in  foreign
