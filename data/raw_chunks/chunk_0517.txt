Freight Holding Supplier Financing Program 
Freight Holding utilizes a third-pa rty financial institution that allows our suppliers to be paid by the third-party financial 
institution earlier than the due date on the applicable invoice at a discounted price. In genera l, supplier invoices financed b y the third-
party financial institution are due for payment by Freight Holding within thirty days. 
As of December 31, 2023 and 2024, the liability related to Freight Holding’s supplier financing program are included within 
accounts payable on the consolidated balance sheets.
