MLU B.V . Investment 
During 2018, we closed a transaction that contributed the net as sets of our Uber Russia/CIS operations into a newly formed 
private limited liability company (“MLU B.V .” or “Yandex.Taxi  joint venture”), with Yandex N.V (“Yandex”) and us holding 
ownership interests in MLU B.V . In excha nge for consideration contribut ed, we received a seat on MLU B.V .’s board and an initia l 
38% equity ownership interest consisting of common stock in MLU B.V . The investment was determ ined to be an  equity method 
investment due to our ability to exercise significant influence over MLU B.V .
