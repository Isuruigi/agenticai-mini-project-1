Market-Based Awards 
We have granted RSUs and stock options that vest only u pon the satisfaction of the following conditions: service-based 
conditions, performance-based conditions, and/or market-based c onditions. The service-based condition for these awards generall y is 
satisfied over three or four years. The performance-based conditions generally are satisfied upon achieving specified performan ce 
targets. The market-based conditions are satisfied upon our achieve ment of specified fully-diluted equity values, as determined  based 
on our stock price.
