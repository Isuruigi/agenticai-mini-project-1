Massachusetts Attorney General Lawsuit 
On July 9, 2020, the Massachusetts Attorney  General filed a complaint in Suffolk Coun ty Superior Court against Uber and Lyft. 
The complaint alleges Drivers are employees, and are entitled to protections under the wage and labor laws. On June 27, 2024, t he 
parties reached an agreement to resolve the matter, and the case was dismissed the same day. In October 2024, we paid into a 
settlement fund and resolved the matter.
